About The Event

Prizes worth: ₹ 2000/-

EVENT DESCRIPTION

EQ-IQ is here with a bang! With this plethora of questions, win amazing prizes. This event will test your knowledge on the Business domain. So do you think you have it in you to win big?

Rules and Regulations

  • Only 2 members per team are allowed.
  • Teams which do not report on time will be disqualified.
  • On the spot entries are allowed.
  • It is mandatory for the participants to carry registration receipts. Teams not carrying the receipts with them will not be allowed to participate.
  • It is mandatory for all the participants to carry their ID cards with them. Only college ID/workplace ID/driver’s license/PAN card/passport are accepted as identity proofs.
  • Questions will be set based on the difficulty level.
  • Any team found using smart phones or gadgets will be disqualified on the spot.
  • A tie breaker will be conducted in case of a tie in any of the rounds.
  • Questions with time limit must be completed within the time slot.
  • Violation of any of the rules of Symbiosis Infotech Campus will lead to a disqualification.
  • In case of any discrepancies, the decision taken by the SCIT Sprout IT team will stand final and no objections will be entertained.
  • Any unethical steps such as distracting other teams or participants, disturbing others during tasks, etc. will lead to direct disqualification.
  • Rules are subject to discretion of the judges & organizing committee.

Assessment Criteria

  • There are 4 different rounds in this particular event in total.
  • Rounds 2, 3, and 4 are subject to eliminations based on the decisions of the judges & the organizing committee.
  • Results & feedback from the judges will be declared after completion of every round.
  • The decision of the judges & the organizing committee will be final and binding.

Other Details

  • Prizes worth: ₹ 2000/-
  • Event Date: 9th September 2017.
  • Reporting time: COMING SOON
  • Event time: COMING SOO
  • Venue: Symbiosis Infotech Campus, Phase – I, Hinjewadi, Pune.
