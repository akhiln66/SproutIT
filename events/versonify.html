About The Event


Entry Fee: FREE


EVENT DESCRIPTION

A poem is a sculpture of voice, story and song which is expected to be short and crisp.

A poem has to be written about your favourite entrepreneur.


Bullet Points:

  • Express your thoughts in the form of a poem, verse or rhyme of your favourite entrepreneur
  • Individual entry

Rules and Regulations

  • The contest is open for online submissions only, through the contest page available on (itelf@scit.edu)
  • Window of the event: 1st September, 2017 07:00 pm to 8th September, 2017 07:00 pm
  • Entries received after the mentioned time frame will not be entertained in any circumstances
  • Every poem must have an appropriate title
  • Submit a manuscript of the poem/poems, relating it directly or indirectly to an entrepreneur
  • The central idea must be linked to the title of the poem
  • Ideas can be abstract with a logical message inspired by the life, work, society contributions etc. of an entrepreneur
  • The poem should not exceed a word limit of 100 words
  • The poem can be in free verse or rhyming, in a traditional form or contemporary – the tone and style of the contest is open, although while submitting poems for the competition, poets should consider that the audience includes families and children
  • Poets can write about any aspect that subtly or overtly relates to the entrepreneur
  • Poets can attach image to illustrate their idea
  • Only English poems will be accepted
  • Entry Fee: FREE
