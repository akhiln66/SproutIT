About The Event

Prizes worth: ₹ 7500/-


Entry Fee: ₹ 500/- Per Team


EVENT DESCRIPTION

Avviare will re-invigorate the spirit of entrepreneurship amongst the students. Participants have to come up with innovative solutions to live problem scenarios faced by startups. They will get an opportunity to interact with startups real-time.


Rules and Regulations

Basic Guidelines

  • Only 2-4 members per team.
  • Reporting Time : 9:00am
  • Participants will be e-mailed a problem statement on their registered email ID.
  • Participants must send their solutions as per the guidelines mentioned in the Problem Statement Email.
  • Teams that are not on time for the event will be disqualified on the spot.
  • It is mandatory to carry the Registration Receipt on the day of the event. Teams not having Registration Receipt will not be allowed to participate in Avviare.
  • Participants if studying in college or working must carry their respective ID cards which will be examined at the entry.
  • Participants are strictly prohibited to enter any out of bound areas of SIC, violating this can lead to direct disqualification.
  • Violation of any Rules of SIC will lead to disqualification of the Participants.
  • Decision made by the SCIT will be final in case of any disputes.

Other Details

  • Team Size: 2-4 members
  • Event Duration: 3 Hours
  • Event Date :- 9th September, 2017
  • Reporting time: 9:00 AM
  • Event time: 10:00 AM-1:00 PM
