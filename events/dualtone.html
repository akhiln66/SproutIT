About The Event


Entry Fee: FREE


EVENT DESCRIPTION

Create an artistic illusion by capturing a live photo frame on a mobile/tablet within a photo.

For reference:-



Participants

One person/Max 2 members in one team.


Guidelines

  • The contest is open for online submissions only, through the contest page available on (itelf@scit.edu)
  • Submissions will not be accepted once the deadline lapses. (Date & time will be specified here)
  • The photograph must not contain obscene, provocative, defamatory, objectionable or inappropriate content.

Evaluation Criteria

  • 40% - Number of Facebook likes
  • 20% - Caption
  • 40% - Judges' ratings
  • Judge’s decision will be final

Rules

  • Only two entries allowed per participant/team
  • You are required to provide a unique title and caption for each image submitted
  • The caption should not exceed 20 words
  • Size of the photo should not exceed 2MB and the image should be in JPEG format
  • Only minor burning, dodging and/or color correction is acceptable, as is cropping
  • High dynamic range images (HDR), collage and stitched panoramas are NOT acceptable
  • Photographs will be accepted as long as they do not constitute copyright infringement or fraud
  • The photograph, in its entirety, must be a single work of original material taken by the contest participant
  • Judge’s decision will be final and binding

Window of the event: 1st September, 2017 07:00 pm to 8th September, 2017 07:00 pm.
